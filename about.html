About PawHome

PawHome is a frontend-only pet adoption website concept created with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Our mission is to help rescue pets find loving homes through a clean and easy browsing experience.

We focus on clear pet profiles, transparent adoption steps, and user-friendly forms to help adopters move confidently through the journey.
